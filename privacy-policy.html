Last Updated: This privacy policy is effective as of the current date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

UCI India Pvt. Ltd. ("UCI India," "we," "our," or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your data when you visit our website https://www.uci-india.com or use our services.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services and improve your experience with UCI India. The types of data we gather include:

a) Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title when you contact us or fill out forms.
  • Details you provide when applying for job opportunities.
  • Feedback, requests, or other communications you send us.

b) Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device information such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Use information including pages visited, time spent on pages, and interactions with our website.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (refer to the Cookies section).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including but not limited to:

  • Responding to your inquiries, service requests, or support needs.
  • Providing and improving our products and services.
  • Sending important updates, service announcements, and promotional information with your consent.
  • Analyzing website usage to enhance user experience.
  • Managing recruitment and career opportunities.
  • Complying with legal requirements and protecting our rights.

Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business and services under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes such as official court requests or court orders.
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or those of our users or others.
  • In connection with business transfers such as mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales.

Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access and breaches.

Your Rights

You have rights to access, correct, delete, and control how your personal data is processed.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information that helps us improve our experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our website.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionalities on our site.

Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include firewalls, encryption, secure servers, and access controls.

While we strive to protect your data, no transmission over the internet or electronic storage method is completely secure. You acknowledge this inherent risk when sharing information with us.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct or update your information.
  • Request deletion of your data where permitted by law.
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

International Data Transfers

If you are accessing our services from outside India, your data may be transferred to and processed in India or other countries where UCI India or its service providers operate. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

UCI India is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data during these transfers. We take all necessary steps to comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations governing international data transfers.

This includes implementing appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses, data processing agreements, or other measures required by law to maintain an adequate level of protection for your information.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites for your convenience. These external sites operate independently of UCI India, and we do not control or endorse their content, practices, or privacy policies.

We encourage you to review the privacy statements of these third parties before submitting any personal information, as their practices may differ from ours.

Children's Privacy

Our services and website are intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age.

If you believe that we may have collected personal data from a child without parental consent, please contact us immediately. We will take prompt steps to delete such information from our records.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or business operations. When we make updates, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

This privacy policy governs your use of our website and services. By using our website or providing information to us, you agree to the terms of this policy.
