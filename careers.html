Salesforce Developer

Salesforce Developer with hands-on experience in Apex, Lightning Web Components (LWC), Visualforce, and Community Cloud. The ideal candidate should be comfortable working on platform customizations, integrating third-party systems, and ensuring code quality through proper testing practices.

Key Requirements:
  • Strong understanding of Salesforce integrations and REST APIs
  • Experience with unit testing and maintaining test coverage
  • Familiarity with Salesforce DX and version control using Git
  • Salesforce certifications (Developer I/II) are strongly preferred

Our Hiring Process

We believe in a hiring process that is simple, transparent, and respectful of your time

From your first application to your first day at UCI, we aim to make the experience smooth and straightforward.

1

Application Review

Once you submit your application, our team carefully reviews your profile to see how well your skills and experience align with the role.

2

Initial Discussion

If your profile is a good match, we'll reach out for an initial conversation. This is a chance for us to get to know you better and for you to learn more about the role and our work culture.

3

Technical/Skill Assessment

Depending on the position, we may ask you to complete a task or assessment to better understand your abilities and how you approach real-world challenges.

4

Final Interview

Candidates who move forward will have a final discussion with our leadership or project team. This stage focuses on your experience, working style, and how well you fit into the team.

5

Offer & Onboarding

If selected, you'll receive a formal offer. Once you accept, we'll guide you through our onboarding process and make sure you have everything you need to get started with confidence.
